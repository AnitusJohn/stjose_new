Diploma in Childhood Education

Early childhood care and education (ECCE) refers to the development of care and education of children between zero and eight years old throughout history. The role of 21st century teachers is evolving everyday with technological revolution seeping into all spheres of life including education and tremendous amount of importance is being given to early childhood education across the globe. The field of Pre-primary education has changed dramatically as educating tiny tots has brought in a bunch of challenges in today’s classrooms along with the rewards. Today’s pre-primary teachers are expected to adapt to their surroundings and foster a unique learning environment that has a positive impact on the children’s all round development.

Job Opportunities

There are opportunities for early childhood or preschool teachers who have complete training in early childhood education and care and have a passion to teach young learners. With the growing popularity of early childhood education, there is a considerable demand for certified teachers. Diploma in Early childhood care and Education (ECCE) opens many avenues to numerous job opportunities as the course aims to provide the future teachers with a strong foundation of the principles of early childhood education and care along with knowledge of planned teaching, offering variety of routes into teaching career.

About the Program

The Early Childhood Care Education and Administration Course is designed to prepare learners to work with children aged 0-8 years. This early childhood education course aims to provide theoretical knowledge and a deeper understanding of core principles that form the foundation of Early Childhood Education, based on child development.

The Early Childhood Care Education and Administration course, one of the most impactful teacher training courses, enables the learners to acquire attitudes and skills to work with children in the 0-8 years of age range using play way methods.

This hybrid and online early childhood education course equips learners to organise, plan, and administer preschool programs while working effectively with parents and the community in supporting their children’s development.

The course covers various aspects of ECCEd&A, including stages of child development, global pioneers and its approaches, storytelling, puppetry, teaching methods, child rights, role of a teacher, child health, hygiene, and nutrition.

This Course is available in two formats:
• A. Face to Face (with access to online materials) called Hybrid
• B. Online (completely online)

Aims and Objectives of the Course

  • The Diploma in Early Childhood Education aims to foster the holistic development of children by equipping teachers with knowledge of the physical, cognitive, emotional, and social aspects of child development.
  • The course promotes a child-centred learning environment that encourages exploration, creativity, and critical thinking among young learners.
  • It trains teachers to create inclusive classrooms where all children—regardless of their background or abilities—feel valued and supported in their learning journey.
  • This ECCE course also encourages aspiring and current educators to pursue a sustainable and fulfilling career in early childhood education.

Who Should Join?

  • Individuals who want to pursue a career in early childhood education as teachers, assistants, or caregivers.
  • Parents or guardians who wish to understand child development better and apply educational principles at home.
  • Educators looking to specialise in early childhood education or transition from teaching older children.
  • Overall, the Diploma in Early Childhood Education course is ideal for anyone who is passionate about making a positive impact on the lives of young children and wants to be equipped with the skills and knowledge to do so effectively.
