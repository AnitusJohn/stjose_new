Managing Director

Dr.Antony Xavier

M.A. M.P.A., M.Phil., M.Ed., Ph.D., LLB

Antony Xavier is the founder and the Managing director of Aldhaas Montessori International (DMI) Education and Training. He is an accomplished Professional - Educationalist/ Administration in the field of education for the past 28 years. His Career complemented with extensive voluntary service through National Service Scheme (NSS) at Higher Secondary School and Graduation levels at Loyola College, Chennai. He is also a member of Association Montessori Internationale (AMI), Amsterdam, Netherlands. He also holds education Records such as M.A.(Hist),M.A.(Pub.Admin), M.Ed., M.Phil, Ph.D in Education. He is qualified from Universities like Madras University, Alagappa University, Annamalai University and Bharathidasan University.

His career commenced in 1994 as a middle school teacher in Holy Cross Matriculation H.S. School, Salem, Tamilnadu and has served as Teacher, Coordinator, Head Teacher, Administrator, Vice-Principal and Principal of schools for 18 years. In the Kingdom of Bahrain he has served as Vice-Principal for The Asian School for two years. He has been an instrument in initiating seven schools across the states of Karnataka and Tamilnadu in India. He has established five Teacher Training Institutes. He also serves as Educational Mentor for Taruwin Vidhya Mandir School, Dharmapuri, Shree Shanthi Anand Vidyalaya, Thirumudivakkam St. Paul’s Group of Schools, Chengalpet, and St. Joseph’s Modern School, Alenvilai, Kanniyakumari.

Antony Xavier has held numerous leadership positions in the field of education both formal and non-formal, and for the rural and street children upliftment. He is a strong believer of “Educating the Heart and Mind” and being inspired by Rev. Fr. Basil Anthony Moreau and Brother Andre Bessettee, the gems of the Congregation of Holy Cross, which involves experiential learning strategies that foster critical thinking and zeal for service.

Mission

Our mission is to empower learners worldwide by providing accessible, high-quality education that fosters innovation, encourages lifelong learning, and builds a supportive and inclusive community. We aim to bridge educational gaps and create opportunities for personal and professional growth through state-of-the-art technology and diverse, engaging content.

Vision

In partnership with the community to provide a safe, nurturing and inspiring learning environment with meaningful learning experiences that meet the highest educational and ethical standards in order to empower every person to become a life-long learner and a responsible, productive and engaged citizen of the world.

Goals

Expand Global Reach: Increase the number of users worldwide by providing multilingual support and localizing content to cater to diverse cultural contexts.

Promote Lifelong Learning: Encourage and support continuous education by offering flexible learning options and resources suitable for all ages and stages of life.

MAIN OBJECTIVES AND GUIDELINES OF THE PROGRAMME:

The Institute strives to educate students in order to train their intellectual, moral, emotional, physical and aesthetic faculties.
  • To provide vocational education and training to the people of all nationalities in Bahrain and GCC countries to fulfil their quest for employment and professional growth.
  • To provide holistic education, relevant to meet the international and national accreditation requirements with high standards by qualified and experienced instructors.
  • To educate students to have active, creative minds with an attitude of enquiry, have a sense of understanding and compassion for others and instil the courage to act on this conviction.
  • To develop unique, creative and vision-focused management and leadership qualities in students.
  • To develop and adopt a trainee-centred approach to promote global citizens capable of succeeding in a world with ever widening horizons.

Importance of Vocational Education

Vocational education is a form of training that prepares individuals for specific trades, crafts, and careers at various levels. Unlike traditional academic education, it is more focused on practical applications and hands-on experience.

Why is Vocational Education Important?

  • Improves the quality of life and promotes moral and ethical values.
  • Contributes to national development and reduction in poverty.
  • Supports industrial, agricultural, and economic growth through technical training.
  • Introduces a variety of technical and career-related subjects.
  • Encourages self-reliance through skill development.
  • Applies scientific knowledge to solve environmental problems.
  • Restructures education systems for better job opportunities.

Benefits of Vocational Education

  • Transforms hobbies into professions with earning potential.
  • Builds specialized skills that are equally important as academic knowledge.
  • Opens wide employment opportunities with better specialization.
  • Fosters self-reliance with job-oriented training.
  • Supports national economy through skilled manpower.
  • Promotes physical activity and healthy living through manual work.
  • Recognized in both Indian and international job markets.

Top Vocational Courses

Fire and Safety Paramedical Management Education Technical Skill Courses

The Growing Scope of Vocational Training

In a skills-driven job market, vocational education plays a vital role in addressing unemployment by equipping individuals with industry-relevant skills. On-the-job training and continual skill development make workers more employable and reduce the need for further training.

Bachelor of Vocational Degree (B.Voc.) Programme

B.Voc is a 3-year undergraduate program focusing on application-based learning across diverse fields like healthcare, hospitality, graphic design, and more. Students benefit from multiple exit options with certifications like Diploma (1 year), Advanced Diploma (2 years), and B.Voc Degree (3 years).

Why Choose B.Voc?

  • Offers industry-relevant and skill-based training.
  • Provides flexibility with exit points.
  • Recognized by UGC and approved by AICTE/HRD/Govt. of India.
  • Prepares students for jobs, research, and entrepreneurship.

Eligibility

12th pass in any stream (Science, Commerce, Arts) or 10th + 2 years of ITI training.

Award Levels

  • Diploma – 1 Year
  • Advanced Diploma – 2 Years
  • B.Voc Degree – 3 Years
